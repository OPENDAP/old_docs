The Distributed Oceanographic Data System:

  • Not a system in the traditional sense

  • Components that provide direct access to oceanographic data over the Internet

  • Driven by science requirements

  • Data maybe stored at many sites ranging from traditional archives to sites with recently gathered data

  • Data may be stored in many different formats





A workshop held in the Fall of 1993 defined the requirements, and suggested several design paths, for DODS. The workshop participants consisted of a mix of oceanographers, data archives maintainers and software developers.
