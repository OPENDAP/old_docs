Research Data

Characteristics:

  • Complex structure
  • Tremendous variability
  • Context dependent
  • Wide range of operations
  • No standardized format

With the growth of the Internet there is a fundamental change in the availability of research data. This:

  • Provides new opportunities and directions for research
  • Increases the amount and type of data that a researcher can access
  • Escalates the complexity of using data
