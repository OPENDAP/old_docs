Using DODS to Access and Deliver Remote Data

6. Collaborators

The initial design and implementation of DODS was undertaken as part of a collaboration between the University of Rhode Island and the Massachusetts Institute of Technology. Since then those actively participating in DODS has grown to include systems developers and scientists from URI, MIT, JPL/PO-DAAC, NOAA/PMEL, NCAR/HAO, UCAR/Unidata, AGU, GSFC/GCMD, OSU, U. Miami, Scripps, and U. Hawaii.
