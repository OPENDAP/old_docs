Known weaknesses of DODS:

  • In many cases the data types defined by the DAP do not provide the flexibility needed by some APIs (e.g., DODS lacks intra dataset pointers).

  • The current model of translation may not prove powerful enough as new APIs are added to the system.

  • The servers do not support the data locator well. Without better support it is hard to imagine the locator living up to its potential.
